Transporting parcels

You can follow the procedure below.

  1. On week days in all railway stations are accepted parcels for transport from 9.00am to 4.00pm
  2. Pack them properly to prevent wastage or damage
  3. If the station master requests you to do so, be prepared to open and display the items in the parcel
  4. Attach the Receiving Person's Name and Address to the Parcel.
  5. Give the Receiving Person's Name and Address and the National Identity Card Number to the Station Master. And also Give the National Identity Card of the Person handing over th Parcel to the Station Master
  6. The Way Bill will be issued to you. This Way Bill is to be sent to the person receiving the Parcel
  7. To collect the Parcel at the destination, give the Way Bill and the National Identity Card of the person receiving the Parcel to the Station Master

Payment

To transport parcels by

  • Express Trains and Semi Express Trains - 2 x Normal Rate
  • Intercity Trains - 3 X Normal Rate
